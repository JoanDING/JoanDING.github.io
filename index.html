Short Bio

Dr. Yujuan Ding is currently Assistant Professor (Research) at School of Fashion & Textiles, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). Before that, she was a Postdoctoral Fellow of the Department of Computing (COMP) under the support of PolyU Distinguished Postoctoral Fellowship., working with Prof. Qing Li and Dr. Wenqi Fan. She received her Ph.D. degree at SFT PolyU in 2021 and visited School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS) in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Tat-Seng Chua. She was recipient of the Best Student Paper Award of ICMR 2021.

Research Interests [Google Scholar]

Data Mining, Information Retrieval and Multimedia Analysis, with a particular focus on:
